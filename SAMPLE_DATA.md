## Pro Tips

1. **Mix Categories**: Add favorites from different categories to show the filtering feature
2. **Use Real Content**: Add videos/content you actually like - makes the demo more authentic
3. **Write Good Descriptions**: Your commentary shows your personality!
4. **Add Tags**: Helps with search functionality
5. **Test Search**: After adding several, test the search feature with different keywords

## Demo Favorites Order

For your demo video, add them in this order:

2. During demo, add one live to show the admin panel
