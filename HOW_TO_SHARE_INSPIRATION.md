## Ways to Share What You're Looking For:

### **Option 1: Describe the Effect** (Easiest)
Just tell me what you see! For example:
- "The background has gradients that shift from left to right"
- "Colors change as you scroll down"
- "There are floating shapes that pulse"
- "The background has a wave-like motion"
- "It looks like liquid or water moving"

### **Option 2: Share Screenshots or GIFs**
- Take screenshots of key moments
- Create a GIF of the effect
- Paste images directly into the chat
- I can see and analyze images!

### **Option 3: Describe Specific Elements**
Tell me about:
- **Motion:** "The gradient moves horizontally" or "Shapes pulse in and out"
- **Colors:** "It goes from purple to pink to blue"
- **Speed:** "It moves slowly" or "It's fast and energetic"
- **Timing:** "It changes when I scroll" or "It's always moving"
- **Style:** "It's subtle" or "It's very bold and vibrant"

### **Option 4: Reference Similar Effects**
Say things like:
- "Like the background in [website/app name]"
- "Similar to Instagram Stories gradient backgrounds"
- "Like those TikTok transitions where colors shift"
- "Like Apple's website with the moving gradients"

### **Option 5: Use Video Links**
If you have YouTube, Vimeo, or other links:
- Share the URL
- Tell me the timestamp (e.g., "at 0:15-0:30")
- Describe what happens in that section
